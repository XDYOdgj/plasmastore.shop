Yoofoss Muslin Swaddle Blankets

Made with a special blend of 70% rayon from bamboo and 30% organic cotton, these swaddle blankets are engineered for the perfect balance of softness, breathability, and safety. Gentle on your baby's skin yet durable enough for everyday use, Yoofoss is designed to meet the needs of modern parents with care and comfort in mind.

Soft & Breathable Fabric

The ultra-soft fabric is three times smoother than regular cotton, offering a touch that's kind to your baby's delicate skin. This breathability helps regulate body temperature, making it ideal for wrapping your little one from head to toe during sleep or bedtime routines.

Safe & Certified

Certified by the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC), these blankets are built to ensure safety, quality, and peace of mind. Their smooth texture and hypoallergenic properties make them a trusted choice for parents who prioritize their baby's well-being.

Versatile & Multi-Use

Whether you're swaddling a newborn, using it as a nursing cover, or covering a stroller, these blankets are designed with adaptability in mind. They work equally well as receiving blankets, burp cloths, car seat covers, and more, adding convenience to every day of parenthood.

Exquisite Packaging

Each 4-pack features neutral-colored blankets that are perfect for baby boys and girls. The elegant and thoughtful packaging makes these blankets a wonderful gift for baby showers or special occasions.

Durable & Easy to Care For

These swaddle blankets are built to last. Their easy-care properties ensure they withstand repeated machine washes without losing color, softness, or shape, making them a practical and long-lasting addition to your baby's care routine.

Perfect for Every Baby

With a gentle, breathable feel and a reliable, safety-focused design, Yoofoss muslin swaddle blankets are the ideal choice for parents who want the best for their little ones. Whether for swaddling, nursing, or everyday use, these blankets deliver comfort, convenience, and care all in one.
