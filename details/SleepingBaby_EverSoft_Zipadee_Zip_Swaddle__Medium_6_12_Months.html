SleepingBaby EverSoft Zipadee-Zip Transition Swaddle

Introducing the perfect sleep solution for your little one. The SleepingBaby EverSoft Zipadee-Zip Transition Swaddle is designed to provide comfort, breathability, and support during those crucial nighttime hours. Made with a special blend of 93% viscose from bamboo and 7% spandex, it offers a soft, lightweight feel that keeps your baby cool in warmer months.

Soft and Breathable Fabric

This swaddle is crafted from eco-friendly bamboo viscose, which is known for its natural softness and breathability. It helps regulate your baby's body temperature and prevents overheating, ensuring a peaceful sleep throughout the night.

Ergonomic Starfish Design

With an ergonomic starfish shape, this swaddle mimics the gentle contours of the womb, giving your baby a secure and cozy feel. The design allows for natural leg movement, supporting proper hip development and encouraging sound sleep.

Double Zipper for Easy Changes

The innovative double zipper system enables quick and easy access during nighttime diaper changes. This feature reduces disruptions and allows for a more relaxed and uninterrupted sleep for your baby.

Snug Fit with Adjustable Sizing

Designed for a snug yet flexible fit, this swaddle ensures your baby stays secure without feeling restricted. It adjusts easily for different body sizes and growing stages, making it ideal for use from 6 to 12 months.

Safe and Secure Zip Guards

The swaddle comes with built-in zipper guards that prevent accidental snags, ensuring added safety for your baby during sleep and daytime use. This is a thoughtful detail that keeps your little one safe while you enjoy a peaceful night's rest.

Transition from Swaddling to Wearable Blanket

This versatile swaddle transitions smoothly from a traditional swaddle to a wearable blanket, making it an essential addition to your baby's sleep routine. It helps your baby gradually adjust from being swaddled to sleeping without, leading to better sleep patterns and longer rest periods.

Whether you're looking to help your baby sleep through the night or transition from swaddling, the SleepingBaby EverSoft Zipadee-Zip Transition Swaddle is the perfect choice. It combines comfort, breathability, and ease of use to support healthy sleep habits for your little one.
