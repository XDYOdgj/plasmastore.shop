Amazing Baby Transitional Swaddle Sack

This transitional swaddle sack is the perfect bridge between traditional swaddling and independent sleep for your little one. Crafted from 100% soft cotton, it ensures a gentle, breathable, and comfortable experience for sensitive baby skin. The unique half-length sleeves with mitten cuffs allow your baby to move naturally while still feeling secure, making it ideal for newborns beginning to roll over or those who prefer to sleep with their arms up.

Designed with an adjustable zipper, this swaddle makes it simple to change diapers and adapt to your baby's growing size. The foldover mittens can be worn open or closed, offering flexibility to prevent facial scratches while encouraging self-soothing. Its snug tummy wrap and shorter leg length support a natural flexed position, promoting better sleep and safer sleep for babies around 3-6 months old.

Whether you're looking for a baby swaddling blanket that adapts to your baby's needs or a cotton swaddle blanket that prioritizes comfort and safety, this transitional swaddle sack is a smart choice. It combines the security of swaddling with the freedom of movement, helping your baby transition smoothly into restful, uninterrupted sleep.
